A La Carte

Link:

Presentation Link

Description:

Worked with Vandan Kolli, Smruthi Srikumar, and Tanya Deepesh to present A La Carte, a service which solves the inefficiencies of the food distribution cycle in megacities. We focused on the city of Mumbai and an example as to how a system such as A La Carte could solve the problems and create a large impact on individual purveyors located in such megacities. We received recognition as a Top Five pitch at the competition held at Rutgers University.
